Card Condition & Quality

We follow industry-standard grading:

  • Near Mint (NM): Appears unplayed with minimal wear
  • Lightly Played (LP): Minor edge wear or light scratches
  • Moderately Played (MP): Noticeable wear but still tournament legal
  • Heavily Played (HP): Significant wear, may have creases
  • Damaged (DMG): Major damage, not tournament legal
