DEFECTIVE/NON-AUTHENTIC/ETC

It is scarce for any of our products to be defective/NON-AUTHENTIC/Damaged. However, we understand that mistakes happen, so if you have any issue with your package or product, you just need to:

  1. Email us - you can find our contact information in the "contact us" section of our website.
  2. Attach a photo or video showing the defect/damage;
  3. Provide the email registered at your purchase, your first and last names and the address that you wish to ship it back to us, in case your request is accepted;
ITEM NEVER ARRIVED

We send you a tracking number at the dispatch stage to the email address you registered when ordering. Our delivery methods can take up to 14 working days; however, in peak times such as Christmas/ holiday seasons and during lockdowns, this can extend by some more days.
