Tournament Information

Everything you need to know about our tournaments

🎯 Tournament Format

  • • Swiss rounds followed by top cut
  • • Best of 3 matches
  • • 40 minute time limit per match
  • • Current TCG banlist enforced

📋 Registration

  • • Register online or at the store
  • • Bring valid deck list
  • • Check-in 30 minutes before start
  • • Late registration may be refused

🏆 Prize Structure

  • • Store credit for top finishers
  • • Booster packs for all participants
  • • Special prizes for monthly events
  • • Loyalty points for regular players

📱 What to Bring

  • • Tournament legal deck (40+ cards)
  • • Extra deck (0-15 cards)
  • • Side deck (0-15 cards)
  • • Pen and paper for life points
